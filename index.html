Antonios Antoniadis


I am an assistant professor at the Discrete Mathematics and Mathematical Programming (DMMP) group at the University of Twente.

My research interests include approximation and online algorithms, energy-efficient algorithms and scheduling theory. I am also very interested in learning augmented online algorithms.

News

  • Our paper Stochastic scheduling with Bernoulli-type jobs through policy stratification, joint with Ruben Hoekma, Kevin Schewior and Marc Uetz was accepted at FOCS 2025!
  • I am on the WAOA 2024 PC.
  • Since January 2024 I am editor of Information Processing Letters (IPL)
